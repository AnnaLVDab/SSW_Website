Ramen is a beloved Japanese noodle soup that has captivated food enthusiasts around the world. This iconic dish consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a flavorful broth, often accompanied by various toppings such as sliced pork, nori (seaweed), menma (bamboo shoots), and green onions. Ramen's versatility and rich flavors make it a popular choice for a comforting meal, whether enjoyed in a bustling ramen shop or at home. With its blend of savory broth, tender noodles, and a variety of toppings, ramen offers a satisfying and customizable dining experience.
